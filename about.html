Ladies Empowerment Organization Workshop 2020

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world” – Nelson Mandela.
LEO is group of Women making the world a better place by encouraging and changing the mindset of women. They have as slogan "Empower the many, mentors the few".
Every year in commemoration of the world day for Women in Tech, they bring women together from across the globe under a single roof. These women interact with each other, share their experiences and encourage each other.
In addition to that, keynote speakers give them a thorough understanding of their personal and professional strengths in the Tech World.
Its a most attend workshop. Feedbacks gotten after these workshops are always so inspiring.

Feel free to email us at ladiesempowermentorg@gmail.com

Our Activities

Leadership Seminars

As a leader of a team the expectations and needs of the people under her care and of the managers above her need to be faced. Leadership seminars are an opportunity to become aware of these and to learn how to competently react to them

Outreach Programs

Programs designed to help and encourage disadvantaged members of the community by providing services to any population that might not otherwise have access to those services.

Online Campaigns

By sensitizing women online, we are making them 'sensitive' about many issues. This is the core of awareness raising and is what we ideally want to achieve – that people become aware and react to certain issues.

Our Yearly Magazine

Our yearly magazine is a digital and print magazine which features our women of substance. We showcase the stories of empowering women who have can have beaten the odds in life and made it to the top
